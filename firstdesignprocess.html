My First Design Process

   Early in college, I was given the opportunity to spend a summer working with one other student to completely redesign the classroom of a popular course called Principles of Engineering. We created a unique design process based off of processes discussed in the d.School's Make Space and the process taught in Olin's course, User-Oriented Collaborative Design. This was my first time working through a design process of any kind, and I quickly realized that it is possible to engineer for people with people. We conducted user interviews early in the summer, and performs codesigns and design reviews with those stakeholders that ultimately engaged with our finished product.
